Overview

<div> <p>This course examines the strategies coastal managers can use to reduce flood and erosion risks, from traditional hard structures to innovative nature-based defence systems.</p> </div>本课程探讨沿海管理者可用于减少洪水和侵蚀风险的策略，从传统硬结构到创新的基于自然的防御系统。

Who should apply

<div> <p>This course is tailored for consultancy and government professionals seeking to deepen their expertise in planning and implementing resilient coastal structures.</p> </div>本课程专为咨询和政府部门专业人士设计，旨在深化其在规划和实施韧性沿海结构方面的专业知识。
