Overview

<div> <p>This course explores the technical links between water and energy systems, from hydropower to energy recovery in treatment plants. Through lectures, case studies, lab work, and field visits, participants learn to analyze and design sustainable, integrated solutions that本课程探讨水与能源系统之间的技术联系，从水力发电到处理厂的能源回收。通过讲座、案例研究、实验室工作和实地考察，学员学习分析和设计可持续的综合解决方案。
