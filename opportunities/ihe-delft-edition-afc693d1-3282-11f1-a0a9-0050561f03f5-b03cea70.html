Overview

<div> <p>Coastal communities face growing threats from erosion, flooding, and sea-level rise. This course teaches professionals and researchers to model and analyze coastal hazards using hands-on exercises with leading software, enabling informed and sustainable interventions.</p沿海社区面临侵蚀、洪水和海平面上升日益严重的威胁。本课程通过使用领先软件进行实践练习，教授专业人士和研究人员建模和分析海岸灾害，从而实现明智且可持续的干预措施。

Who should apply
