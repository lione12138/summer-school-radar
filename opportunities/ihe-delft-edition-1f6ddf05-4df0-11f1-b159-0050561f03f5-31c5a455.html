Overview

This short course&nbsp;promotes practical use of the Handbook on Emergency Management for Flood Defences while creating learning and networking opportunities for professionals from government, industry and academia. The course serves as the second component of a two-part series,本短期课程促进《防洪应急管理手册》的实际应用，同时为来自政府、行业和学术界的专业人士创造学习和交流机会。该课程是两部分系列课程的第二部分。

Who should apply

Professionals and academics active in flood risk and emergency management (coastal, river and estuarine managers and engineers, flood defence owners, civil/hydraulic/geotechnical engineers, crisis managers, crisis commun从事洪水风险和应急管理的专业人士和学者（包括海岸、河流和河口管理人员及工程师、防洪设施所有者、土木/水利/岩土工程师、危机管理人员、危机沟通人员等）
